About Us

There is one word that describe us " Trustworthy"

According to phone scammers issues in our society, where a lot of people lost their money due to scamers who lied to them telling them that they are MTN or Airtel Staffs or Agents.

After seeing how citizen among our society are struggling and claimming about the the scammers, we came up with the solution, and name it "SAFETY CALL", this will be a mobille application where once you installed and scammer call you saying he/she is a MTN or Aiterl agent or staff, the App will detect it and if it's not a registered number in MTN or Airtel database as a staff number it will automatically be call ended.

these are crucial roles we play for our clients:

  • Providing help to those with emergency situation specifically related to security of their phone calls.
  • facilitating and providing network between a client and call source providers(MTN & Airtel).
